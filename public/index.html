Chrome Extension

Kalshi Intelligence Layer

A research sidebar for Kalshi that shows market data, news context, related markets, price alerts, and multi-outcome analysis — all without leaving the page.

Download Extension

Installation (2 min)

1

Download & Unzip

Click the download button above and unzip the file

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Go to chrome://extensions in your browser

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle "Developer mode" in the top right corner

4

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the unzipped folder

5

Visit Kalshi

Go to any market on kalshi.com — the panel appears automatically

Features

Market Intelligence

Live probability, volume, open interest, bid/ask spread, and price history chart

Multi-Outcome View

See all outcomes at once with probability sum and arbitrage detection

News Context

Relevant headlines pulled automatically, with optional AI summaries

Related Markets

Discover correlated markets in the same event or series

Price Alerts

Get notified when markets cross your target price or move sharply

Thesis Tracking

Record your reasoning and what would change your mind, per market
