Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2025

Kalshi Intelligence Layer ("the Extension") is a browser extension that enhances the Kalshi.com trading experience. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Summary: All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following data in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local):

  • User Preferences: Panel width, theme, block order, and visibility settings
  • Thesis Notes: Your personal notes and probability estimates for each market
  • Price Alerts: Alert configurations you create
  • Cached Data: Temporary market data and news headlines (auto-expires)
  • API Key (optional): If you enable AI summaries, your Anthropic API key is stored locally

This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly enable the optional AI summary feature.

External Services

The Extension connects to the following external services:

  • Kalshi API (api.elections.kalshi.com): Fetches public market data (prices, volume, etc.). No authentication or personal data is sent.
  • Google News RSS (news.google.com): Fetches public news headlines related to market topics. No personal data is sent.
  • Anthropic API (api.anthropic.com) - Optional: If you enable AI summaries and provide your own API key, news headlines are sent to generate summaries. Your thesis notes are never sent.

Data We Don't Collect

  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't track your browsing activity
  • We don't use analytics or tracking scripts
  • We don't have user accounts or servers
  • We don't sell or share any data

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Save your preferences and notes locally
  • notifications: Send price alert notifications
  • alarms: Check prices periodically for alerts
  • activeTab: Detect when you're on a Kalshi market page
  • Host permissions: Access Kalshi pages and fetch data from APIs listed above

Data Deletion

To delete all stored data, uninstall the Extension from chrome://extensions. You can also clear specific data from the Extension's options page.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
